# Locartefact

Locartefact is a personal, iPhone-first app that lets me turn on location tracking and receive short, educational facts about nearby landmarks, natural features, and broader geographic context. Facts are delivered through notifications and stored in a simple feed.

## Core Experience

1. Open the app and turn on tracking.
2. The app detects your location.
3. It identifies nearby notable places and broader geographic context.
4. It ranks potential facts by relevance.
5. It sends a concise notification with the top fact.
6. All delivered facts are stored in a chronological feed.

## Example Notifications

- "You're near Mauna Kea, one of the best astronomical observation sites in the world."
- "Nearby: ʻIolani Palace is the only official royal palace in the United States."
- "You're standing on the flank of Mauna Loa, the largest volcano on Earth by volume."
